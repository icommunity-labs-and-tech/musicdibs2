# CONTEXT.md — MusicDibs

## About this project

MusicDibs is a **SaaS platform for independent musicians** built by iCommunity Labs.

It is the all-in-one platform for independent artists: blockchain music registration, worldwide distribution, rights management, royalties, and direct sales.

**Tagline:** "Your work, yours alone."

---

## What MusicDibs does

MusicDibs solves three problems for independent artists:

1. **Legal protection** — blockchain-certified registration of music works with international legal validity
2. **Distribution** — release music on 220+ digital platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, etc.)
3. **Monetization** — earn royalties, sell works in the marketplace, access marketing and promotion services

---

## Core features

### Legal protection
Every registration generates an immutable digital certificate backed by blockchain, valid as legal proof of authorship. Compliant with Berne Convention, WIPO Treaty, EU Copyright Directive, and blockchain IP regulations. Instant registration — seconds, not weeks, no bureaucracy.

### Distribution
- 220+ DSPs (Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, and more)
- Earn royalties from the first stream
- Real-time statistics
- No hidden fees

### Marketplace
Sell music works directly to buyers via the MusicDibs Market.

### Social media promotion
Promotion services via MusicDibs social channels (TikTok 245k+ · Instagram 100k+ followers).

### AI features
- AI article generator (content marketing for artists)
- AI music generation (future direction — accepting AI-generated works already)
- No censorship: accepts AI-generated and experimental works (with full rights held)

### Dashboard
- Credit system for registrations
- Activity charts
- Credit store and history
- Account management

## Pricing model

**Basic plan (monthly):**
- Up to 3 registrations/month
- Continuous certificate access
- Signup fee (first month only)

**Plus+ plan (annual):**
- Up to 120 registrations/year (no extra cost)
- Full distribution on 220+ DSPs
- 2 social media promotions/month
- MusicDibs Market access
- Priority support + dispute assistance
- 4 months free on annual subscription

**Individual registration:** also available per work (~€11.90)

**B2B / White-label:** available for professionals, companies, academies.

## Competitive positioning

Direct competitors: DistroKid · CD Baby · TuneCore

MusicDibs differentiator: combines **blockchain legal protection + distribution + marketplace + no censorship** in a single platform. Competitors focus on distribution only.

Up to **95% royalties** retained by the artist.
